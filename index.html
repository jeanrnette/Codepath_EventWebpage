UPCOMING EVENTS

Tournament: Friday, [Month/Day]

Join us for an exciting and competitive Bicycle Cards Tournament, where players can showcase their skills in various card games, from strategy-based classics to fast-paced challenges. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the game, this tournament promises fun and fierce competition! Test your abilities, challenge friends, and win amazing prizes while enjoying the company of fellow card enthusiasts. With multiple rounds, exciting challenges, and a grand prize at the end, this is an event you won't want to miss!

An afternoon of fun!

  • Games: Solitaire, Spades, Rummy, Crazy Eights, etc...
  • Prizes: $50 gift card to restaurant/game-store of choosing, Custom card deck, Card-themed hoodie with club logo
  • Food: Pizza (including vegan options), Chips and dip station, desserts

event schedule

check-in

12:00pm-12:30pm

Sign in and see who you're up against. Participants arrive, confirm your RSVP, and get your table numbers. Meet other players and warm up with a casual game.

round 1

12:30pm-1:30pm

Players compete in solo games at different tables. The top scorers from each game will move on to the 1v1 finals. Scoring is based on performance and sportsmanship.

lunch

2:30pm-3:15pm

A casual lunch with music and snacks, giving players a chance to socialize, talk strategy, and relax before the final round.

final round

3:15pm-4:30pm

The best players from round 1 will go head-to-head in 1v1 battles. Winners of each match will compete in a final table showdown for the top prize.

closing ceremony

5:00pm-5:30pm

Well announce winners, hand out prizes, and take a group photo. Everyone walks away with memories, good vibes, and possibly a new favorite card game!
